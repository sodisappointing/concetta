Amenities

Sitting Room

Unwind in our cozy sitting room. Relax on the couch and bathe in the soothing natural light. Enjoy your favorite shows and movies in cozy ambiance on the flatscreen TV with Hulu and Netflix.

Creekside Terrace

Escape to the tranquil Creekside Terrace, where the sound of gently flowing water provides a soothing soundtrack to contemplation or conversation. With direct access to the creek, this serene enclave is perfect for fishing, kayaking, or simply relaxing and taking in the natural beauty surrounding the Concetta De.

Outdoor Pavilion

Our Outdoor Pavilion offers a delightful space to gather and enjoy the outdoors. With ample seating and an outdoor cooking area, it's the perfect setting for al fresco meals, gathering with friends or family, or simply enjoying the fresh air.

Other Amenities

  • Ample parking. Boats, campers, and motorcyclists welcome!
  • Wrap-around porch.
  • Breakfast served at 8:30 every day. Early, late, or grab-and-go breakfast available upon request.
  • Heat and air conditioning in every room.
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi.
  • Two common bathrooms with choice of jetted tub or walk-in shower.
  • Host resides on premises.
