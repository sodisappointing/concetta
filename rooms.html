Rooms

Each room at the Concetta De offers its own unique combination of historic charm and modern amenities, ensuring a stay that's both enchanting and comfortable. From the regal Anne suite to the cozy Kathy room, discover the perfect space to make your stay unforgettable. Breakfast is included with each room. Be sure to review our policies and check out our list of amenities.

Book Your Stay

Anne

The Ann Suite is the ultimate blend of Victorian charm and modern comfort. The metal-tiled ceiling, original hardwood floors, and the king sized antique brass bed capture the essence of a bygone era. Stay cool and comfortable with the convient remote-controlled AC. Pamper yourself with the luxurious master bath, which boasts a walk-in shower, soothing jetted tub, elegant dual sinks, and a smart mirror. Bask in the natural light from the many windows while you take in the view of the rolling hills. Located on the second floor.

$139 per night

Beulah

Beulah is gorgeous and unique with its original wood floors, metal-tiled ceiling, and locally-sourced wood paneling. The neutral colors of nature are warm and inviting, while the king-sized brass bed provides the perfect place to rest. Sit on the loveseat to read and relax or just rock in the rocking chair by the large windows. Choose a modern walk-in shower or a relaxing bath in the jetted slipper tub in the two common bathrooms just down the hall. Located on the second floor.

$119 per night

Kathy

Step back in time with the exposed brick and original wood floors of the late 1800's. Rest comfortably on the elegant queen-sized poster bed or read by the warm light in the large chair. Enjoy a view of the 2-story pavilion and backyard. Choose a modern walk-in shower or a relaxing bath in the jetted slipper tub in the two common bathrooms just across the hall. Located on the second floor.

$109 per night

Pauline

Enjoy the view of the creek side terrace and side yard through the many tall windows of Pauline. Surround yourself in the light colors and florals of this dainty room. Rest comfortably on the double bed on the lower level. Pauline features an attached private bathroom with a walk-in shower. Located on the first floor.

$119 per night

Dana

This bright and cheery room is sure to leave you well-rested and revived. The plush and comfy king-sized bed and two chairs provide a glorious view of the creek and terrace from the second floor. Get ready for the day seated at the vanity or use it as a dedicated workspace. Choose a modern walk-in shower or a relaxing bath in the jetted slipper tub in the two common bathrooms just down the hall. Located on the second floor.

$119 per night
