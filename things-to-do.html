Fun

Up The Creek Winery

A wine connoisseur's delight! Hand-crafted wines from home-grown fruits. Tastings on Fridays and Saturdays from 10 to 6.

Dale Hollow Lake State Resort Park

Less than 20 miles away! Dining, boat ramp, over 15 miles of hiking trails, nationally ranked golf course, and the finest fishing in the state!

Wolf Creek National Fish Hatchery

Environmental Education Center, nature trail, and, of course, fishing!

Fishing

Burkesville is known as the "Fishing Capital of Kentucky", and is home to state record trout and the world record smallmouth bass.

The Beauty Spot Salon and Spa

Full service salon and day spa for men and women. Massages, manicures, pedicures, hair.

Food

Annie Ruby's Cafe

Cafe serving breakfast and lunch. Sweet treats and Southern classics.

El Rey Mexican Restaurant

A little taste of Mexico right in Burkesville.

Mike's Landing

Waterfront dining at Dale Hollow Lake.

Family Tradition

Good old fashioned Southern comfort food.

Festivities

River Festivus

Multi-day community event celebrating life on the Cumberland River. Music, fishing, paddle races, and more! Third weekend every August.

Cumberland River Bluegrass Festival

Come hear the finest bluegrass around. Not just music, but plenty of activities, too! Held in conjunction with the Cumberland County Quilt Show. Third weekend every September.

First Fridays

Cruise-in, food trucks, vendors, music, kid's activities, and more! The first friday of each month from June to September.
